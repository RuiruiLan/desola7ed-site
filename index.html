About

I started producing around 2020 — a teenage kid who liked listening to Puppet, Jaron, and Porter Robinson. I loved playing around with synths and distortion.

I was lucky enough to be discovered by people who appreciated my amateur creations and gave my musical identity meaning. I've made some friends along the way, and lost some over the years as I lost focus and burned out.

Please do not hesitate to reach out and connect. I will find my way back someday.
